About UniQ

UniQ is a revolutionary Q&A platform designed exclusively for college students and faculty. Our mission is to create a vibrant academic community where knowledge is shared freely and collaboratively.

Our Vision

We believe that every student deserves access to quality academic support. UniQ bridges the gap between students across different years, departments, and even institutions, creating a powerful network of shared knowledge.

Platform Features

  • Department-specific question boards
  • Verified faculty responses
  • Peer-to-peer learning environment
  • Resource sharing and collaboration tools
  • Academic integrity safeguards
